Data Deletion Policy

How Facebook Auction Manager handles user data removal requests

We only store the minimum information required to operate your Facebook auction flows. If you would like us to delete data that has been collected through the Facebook Auction Manager platform, follow the steps below.

Self-service deletion

If you are currently logged into the dashboard, open the account settings panel and choose Delete account data. We will immediately remove all inventory, auction drafts, and Facebook access tokens linked to your profile.

Manual request

Alternatively, you can submit a data deletion request by contacting our support team:

Email: support@trackerapp-sys.com

Include the email address or Facebook ID associated with your account so we can locate the right records. We will confirm completion within 7 business days.

Facebook users without an account

If you previously authorised Facebook Auction Manager but never created a dashboard account, send us your Facebook profile ID at the email above and we will revoke stored tokens and purge any cached group listings.

Retention

Backup snapshots are retained for 30 days. Once the deletion request is fulfilled, your data will be removed from active systems immediately and from backups within the retention window.
